Kizhi Parotta

It is a special dish of South India. Here, the parottas are soaked in a semi thick chicken or meat gravy and wrapped in a banana leaf. It is then slow cooked for 2 to 3 minutes in coconut oil.




    Ingredients:


  • Banana Leaf - 1
  • Parottas - 3 to 5
  • Coconut Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Chicken Gravy - as needed
  • Onion - little (chopped)
  • Green Chilli - 1 or 2 (chopped)
  • Coriander Leaves - little (chopped)


    Directions:


  1. Firstly, heat the banana leaf on low flame on all sides.
  2. So that the leaf does not tear while wrapping it.
  3. Now place around 2 or 3 parottas on the leaf, pour chicken gravy as needed.
  4. Place the remaining parottas on top and pour enough gravy.
  5. Garnish with onion, green chilli and coriander leaves.
  6. Now wrap the leaf carefully and tie it.
  7. Heat little coconut oil on a tawa or non stick pan.
  8. Place the wrapped leaf and cover with a lid completely covering the wrapped leaf.
  9. Toast it on low flame for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Remove from flame and serve hot.
