Butter-Chicken

Butter Chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world. Aromatic golden chicken pieces in an incredible creamy curry sauce,

this Butter Chicken recipe is one of the best you will try! You will love how easy it is to make in the comfort of your own home, especially with garlic butter rice and fresh homemade Naan bread.



    Ingredients:


  • 1 tablespoon oil, butter
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger finely minced or grated, 1 teaspoon chilli powder, adjust to taste, 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup heavy cream sub for half & half or yogurt for low fat
  • Hot cooked rice and naan for serving
  • 2-3 cloves garlic finely minced or crushed
  • 1 ½ pounds about 2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into ¾-inch chunks
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste or 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala

    Directions:


  1. Heat a large skillet or medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions down until lightly golden, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add ginger and garlic and let cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn’t burn.
  4. Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices.
  5. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until everything is cooked through.
  6. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve over Basmati rice or with naan.
