Gajar ka Halwa

Carrot Halwa is a popular Indian dessert made with carrots, milk, sugar and ghee. Carrot Halwa also known as Gajar ka Halwa is a classic sweet dish that is often prepared during festivals, special occasions, and celebrations in India.



    Ingredients:


  • 1 kg carrot
  • ¼ cup ghee / clarified butter
  • 10 cashew / kaju, chopped
  • 10 almonds / badam, chopped
  • 3 cup milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ½ cup khoya / mawa
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder


Directions:


  1. firstly, peel the skin of carrot and grate finely.
  2. keep aside. traditionally, delhi carrot is used to prepare halwa.
  3. in a large kadai heat ¼ cup ghee and fry 10 cashew, 10 almonds.
  4. fry until it turns golden brown. keep aside.
  5. in the same ghee add grated carrot and saute well.
  6. saute for 5 minutes or until it changes colour slightly.
  7. now pour 3 cup milk and give a good stir.
  8. boil for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  9. continue to boil until the carrots are cooked well and milk reduces.
  10. once the milk thickens completely, add ¾ cup sugar.
  11. mix well and cook until the sugar dissolves and thickens.
  12. cook until the halwa thickens and ghee releases from sides.
  13. now turn off the flame and ½ cup khoya, ¼ tsp cardamom powder and fried nuts.
  14. mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  15. finally, enjoy gajar ka halwa or carrot halwa chilled or warm.
