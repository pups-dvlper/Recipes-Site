Dragon Chicken

Crispy chicken strips, onion, capsicum and cashews tossed in a sweet and spicy thick sauce make this Indo-Chinese appetizer extremely delicious. An ideal side dish for lunch or dinner, this recipe is a crowd pleaser.



    Ingredients:


  • 350 gram Chicken breast strips, 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper, to taste Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder, 1 tbsp Rice flour
  • 1 tbsp Maida
  • 1 tbsp Garlic (finely chopped), 1 tsp Red chilli sauce
  • 1 Medium onion (cut into chunks), 1 Medium red capsicum (cut into chunks)
  • 1/4 cup Cashew nuts, 1/2 tsp Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce, 1 tsp Tomato ketchup
  • to taste Salt, 1 tsp Corn flour
  • 1/4 cup Water, Medium green capsicum (cut into chunks)
  • Spring onions (for garnishing)


    Directions:


  1. For the marinade: Add all the ingredients into a bowl. Mix well and set aside. Make sure there are no lumps.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, for shallow frying the marinated chicken strips.
  3. Now, add one chicken strip at a time and allow them to fry on medium heat until golden brown.
  4. For the stir fry: Heat oil in a wok on high heat. Add garlic and red chilli paste. Fry on high heat for 1-2 mins.
  5. Add onion, capsicum and sautee well.
  6. Add vinegar, soya sauce, ketchup and salt. Mix well.
  7. In a separate bowl, combine corn flour and water. Add it to the wok. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  8. Add fried chicken strip
